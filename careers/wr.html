ABOUT US

Wicked Ride, one of India's largest bike rental companies with a presence across 13+ cities and New Zealand. Over the years, Wicked Ride has clocked over 10 million km and catered to over 2.5 lakh customers since inception. Along with Wicked Ride, we run another brand alongside called Metro Bike. Wicked Ride caters to the premium segment dealing with bikes such as Harley Davidson, Ducati, Honda, KTM, Royal Enfield etc and Metro Bikes caters to the utility segment dealing with the last minute commute problems faced by commuters.
